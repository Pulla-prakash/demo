Vcare Health City,

For over 32 years, Apollo Health City, Hyderabad, has touched millions of lives, providing care and treatment for the simple to the most complicated diseases and conditions.

Today, Apollo Hospitals, Hyderabad is redefining healthcare excellence by combining the best available technology and medical expertise to ensure patients receive comprehensive and integrated services, including the utmost compassion, support and care.

Recognized as a world-renowned facility, Apollo Health city Hyderabad not only provides outstanding care and treatment, but constantly betters clinical outcomes using Information Technology, comprehensive research programs and education all in one sprawling campus, creating an environment dedicated to healing.

Highlights

  • Featuring the very latest technology, much of it not available anywhere else in the region, the stunning 550-bed multi-specialty hospital with over 50 specialties and super-specialties and 12 Centres of Excellence, covers the entire spectrum of health from illness to wellness.
  • The Centres of Excellence for Heart, Critical Care, Neurosciences, Cancer, Emergency, Orthopaedics, Gynaecology, ENT, Transplants, Gastroenterology, Pulmonology and Colorectal at Apollo Hospitals continue to serve the community by providing the highest quality of personalised patient care. Apart from patient care, each of these Centres of Excellence spend significant time in training and research, essentially aimed at preventing disease and improving outcomes when disease does occur.
  • Most of the specialist consultant doctors at the Apollo Hospitals have globally recognised experience and expertise to treat innumerable diseases and conditions.

The hospital is guided by the values of Apollo Hospitals Group and is striving hard to achieve the Group’s vision to ‘Touch a Billion Lives’. We are committed to achieve this vision and build a positive, safe, patient-focused culture.

For more details on Apollo hospitals and clinics at Hyderabad, visit https://hyderabad.apollohospitals.com/
